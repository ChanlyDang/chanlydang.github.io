The Process for my Projects

My First Game: Time's Up Worm

Click above to play my first finished game! It's nothing special, but I'm proud of it!

This game and its assets were made by me in Godot. Fonts were provided by Google fonts. I made the music using Bosca Ceoil. The pixel art character and background were made in Gimp. Tiles were arranged using Tilesetter.

My Second Game: Friends in You

Click above to play my second playable game!

This game was made using the Twine Game engine, which is specifically for interactive fiction type games. It's based on HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I did not create the border, but credit to the original creator. This game was made for my Digital Storytelling class, then I edited it slightly to fix minor issues.
